Solar-powered irrigation solutions that save water and reduce electricity costs. Perfect for vegetable farms, crop production, and landscaping.

System Types:

  • Drip Irrigation: Water-efficient system ideal for row crops and vegetables
  • Sprinkler Systems: Suitable for lawns, gardens, and smaller fields

  • Greenhouse Irrigation: Automated systems for controlled environments

What's Included:

  • Land survey and system design

  • Automated timer and control system
  • Training on system operation

Water Pumps

Solar-powered water pumps for boreholes, rivers, and reservoirs. Reliable solutions for drinking water, livestock watering, and irrigation.

Pump Types:

  • Submersible Pumps: For deep boreholes (up to 200 meters)
  • Surface Pumps: For rivers, dams, and shallow wells
  • Booster Pumps: For increasing water pressure in existing systems
  • Floating Pumps: For reservoirs and large water bodies

What's Included:

  • Borehole assessment and water yield testing
  • Solar panel array and pump installation
  • Water storage tank and piping (optional)
  • 5-year pump warranty

Price Range: Starting from R18,000 for small submersible pumps

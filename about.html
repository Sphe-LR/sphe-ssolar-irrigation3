About Sphe's Solar and Irrigation

Founded in 2015 by Sphe Mnisi, a renewable energy technician who saw the need for affordable solar solutions in rural Mpumalanga. Starting as a one-person operation installing solar home systems, the company expanded into water irrigation in 2018 after repeated requests from local farmers. Today, Sphe's employs 12 full-time staff and has completed over 500 installations across Mpumalanga, Limpopo, and Gauteng.

🎯 Our Mission

Empower South African families and farmers with affordable solar energy and water security.

🔭 Our Vision

A future where every farm is energy-independent and climate-resilient.

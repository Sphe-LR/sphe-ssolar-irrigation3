About Sphe's Solar & Irrigation

Empowering South Africa with sustainable energy and water solutions since 2015.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Sphe Mnisi, a renewable energy technician who saw the need for affordable solar solutions in rural Mpumalanga. Starting as a one-person operation installing solar home systems, the company expanded into water irrigation in 2018 after repeated requests from local farmers.

Today, Sphe's employs 12 full-time staff and has completed over 500 installations across Mpumalanga, Limpopo, and Gauteng. We are proud to be a SAPVIA certified installer and a trusted partner for sustainable farming solutions.
